Current Squad

The current Springboks squad features a blend of experienced veterans and exciting young talent, showcasing the depth and strength of South African rugby. Key players include captain Siya Kolisi, a dynamic flanker known for his leadership and work rate; Eben Etzebeth, a powerful lock with a formidable presence in the lineout; and Handré Pollard, a fly-half with exceptional kicking skills and game management. The squad also boasts the pace and skill of wingers Cheslin Kolbe and Makazole Mapimpi, who have been instrumental in the team's attacking prowess. With a strong forward pack and a creative backline, the current Springboks squad is well-equipped to compete at the highest level of international rugby.
